Our services

Professional grooming is an important part of enhancing your pet's quality of life.

Happy Paws offers a wide range of services that will leave your pet clean and refreshed. If you want to give your pet the royal treatment, we have delightful shampoos, facials, and other treatments to pamper your pet. When you arrive at Happy Paws our stylists put your pet first. We make sure we get to know your pet, understand any special needs they may have, and consult with you on the services and style you would like. We provide a quieter and less chaotic environment to ensure your pet is comfortable and less stressed during their grooming experience.
